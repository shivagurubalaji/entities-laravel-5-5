National Australia Bank Ltd (NAB.xasx) Profile

National Australia Bank Limited is an Australian financial services company including segments such as Business and Private Banking. NAB's, through its segments, provide specialized agriculture, business franchise, education, government, health, and community services. NAB is one of the four largest financial institutions in Australia, with 35,063 employees as of 2016. The National Australia Bank stock (NAB) is listed in Australian Securities Exchange Ltd (ASX), and the market capitalisation was $ 93.77B (AUD) as of November 2021. As of January 2019, NAB operated 3,500 Bank@Post locations—including 7,000+ ATMs across Australia, New Zealand, and Asia—and served 9 million customers. National Australia Bank plans to acquire neobank 86 400 for $ 220M (AUD). In the third quarter of 2021, the company's cash profit rose 10.3 per cent to $ 1.70B (AUD).
