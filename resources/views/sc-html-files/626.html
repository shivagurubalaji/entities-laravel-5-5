Blink Charging Co (BLNK.xnas) Profile

Blink Charging Co (BLNK.xnas) is a leading company provider of electric vehicle (EV) charging equipment and services headquartered in Miami, California. Blink Charging, design, manufacture, own, and operate EV charging stations of almost 30,000 in number in 13 countries, many of which are networked EV charging stations, allowing EV drivers to charge at any of the Company’s charging locations globally. The company has established key strategic partnerships for rolling out adoption across various locations, health care facilities, schools and universities, airports, auto dealers, hotels, parks, and more to raise their purchases to 10M vehicles by 2025. Blink Charging Co (BLNK.xnas) stock is traded on the NASDAQ, had a market capitalisation of $1.44B (USD) as of December 2021 and a $6.4M (USD) in 2021 revenue. Blink Charging collaborated with SG Blocks, Inc. to deploy Blink’s EV charging equipment and network via SG Blocks’ modular designs. The company announced the opening of a new office in Noida, India, adjacent to New Delhi that would offer innovative new software capabilities to meet better the needs of an evolving EV charging landscape and increase operations serving in the Asia Pacific and the Middle East regions.
