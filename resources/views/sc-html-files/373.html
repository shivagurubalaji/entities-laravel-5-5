Heineken NV Profile

Heineken NV is a Dutch multinational brewing company headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. HEINEKEN owns and operates over 165 breweries, malteries, cider plants, and other production facilities in more than 70 countries. The Dutch brewing company produces 348 different beers and ciders from all over the world. Heineken NV employs 85,853 people and owns an international portfolio of more than 170 beer brands. Heineken NV (HEIA.xams) is listed on Euronext NV as an Amsterdam Exchange Index (AEX) component and has $52.28B (USD) in market cap. As of September the 15th, 2021, HEINEKEN owns a net worth of $60.84B (USD), while its 2020 revenue has dropped to €20B (EUR), a 16.11% decrease compared to 2019s’ €24B (EUR) sales. Most recently, the company’s sales surge as hospitality reopening shows encouraging signs for the brewing industry and an expected spark on beer demand. In the United Kingdom, Heineken NV currently announced a £38M (GBP) cash injection into its pub and bar businesses around the country.
