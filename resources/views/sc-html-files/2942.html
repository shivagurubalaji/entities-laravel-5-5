Cabot Oil & Gas Corp (COG) Profile

A subsidiary of Cabot Oil & Gas Corp (Cabot), the company is a standalone oil and gas company that develops and explores for oil and gas in its holdings mostly located in the United States. The company's primary focus is on the development of natural gas resources in the Marcellus Shale formation in northeast PA. It primarily owns and runs assets in Susquehanna County, Pennsylvania. Natural gas marketers, industrial clients, distribution centers businesses, and power manufacturing facilities are among the company's natural gas customers. Cabot Transport transfers natural gas via third-party gathering systems and interstate pipelines, among other modes of transportation. It conducts business through regional and district offices in Texas and Pennsylvania, among other places. Cabot is Headquartered in the United States of America.
