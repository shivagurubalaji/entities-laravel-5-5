Booking Holdings Inc Profile

Booking Holdings Inc (BKNG.xnas) is an American travel technology company headquartered in Connecticut, the United States. BOOKING operates through several travel fare platforms and travel fare metasearch engines, including Booking.com, Priceline.com, Cheapflights, Rentalcars.com, Momondo, Kayak.com, and OpenTable. Booking Holdings Inc’s online services are available in 43 languages and 225 countries, offering access to over 28 million reported accommodation listings in 148,470 destinations from 228 countries. Booking.com is currently the second-largest travel technology company by the number of website visits, has 13,000 employees, and has $96.26B (USD) in market cap. Booking Holdings Inc (BKNG.xnas) is listed on NASDAQ as a NASDAQ-100 Index component, Standard & Poor’s 100 (S&P-100), and Standard & Poor’s 500 (S&P-500) constituent. BOOKING is heavily affected by the pandemic, announcing a $6.79B (USD) annual revenue in 2020, a 54.92% decrease compared to 2019s company results. Following a general optimism about resumptions of the travel industry during summer 2021, Booking Holding Inc’s share price (BKNG.xnas) has shown some marks of recovery. BKNG.xnas’s share price is expected to remain volatile except if the percentage of vaccinated people continues to grow.
