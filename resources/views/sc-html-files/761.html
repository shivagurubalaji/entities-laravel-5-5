BYD Co Ltd Profile

BYD Co Ltd (1211.xhkg) is a Chinese automotive manufacturing company headquartered in Guangdong, the People’s Republic of China. BYD has two major subsidiaries, BYD Automobile and BYD Electronic. BYD Co Ltd produces ordinary passenger cars, commercial vehicles, and other products. BYD is most notable for its full-electric and hybrid cars, buses, trucks, as well as for its battery-powered bicycles, forklifts, solar panels, and rechargeable batteries (mobile phones, electric vehicles, and renewable bulk storage). It is traded on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (SEHK), the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (SZSE) and its market cap are currently ¥735.68B (CNY). BYD Co Ltd employs 229,154 people and has over 30 industrial parks and production bases worldwide. BYD Co Ltd (1211.xhkg) sold 18,711 electric passenger cars in May 2021 and 46,554 units in total for the whole year, 126% more than in May 2020.
