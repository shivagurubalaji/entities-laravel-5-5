Cronos Group Inc (CRON) Profile

Cronos Group Inc. is a cannabis producer. It manufactures, promotes, and sells hemp-derived nutrition and aesthetic goods in the United States through e-commerce, retail, and leisure channel partners. Additionally, the company cultivates, manufactures, and promotes cannabis and cannabis-derived products for medicinal and adult-use markets. It offers cannabis and cannabis products, such as dried cannabis, pre-rolls, and concentrates, across wholesale and direct-to-client channels through its wellness network, PEACE NATURALS; and runs two adult-use brands, COVE and Spinach.
