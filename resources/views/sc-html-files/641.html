Chunghwa Telecom Co Ltd (ADRs) (CHT) Profile

Chunghwa Telecom Co., Ltd. is a Taiwanese telecommunications company. It works through the following segments: Residential Fixed Communications, Mobile Communications, Internet Communications, Global Fixed Communications, and Many others. The company provides local and long-distance telephone and leased vital services, as well as internet access, Wi-Fi, and multimedia on demand; information and communication technology (ICT) services; and connections with other mobile and fixed-line operators via its fixed-line network. Additionally, it offers mobile; HiNet Internet, data communication, and cloud services; applications value-added and Internet data center services; overseas long-distance telephone, leased line, and data services; and satellite communication. Additionally, the company distributes and sells cellular phones, tablets, wearable devices, data cards, telecommunications equipment, ICT products, and computing and business industrial equipment systems and technology; designs, develops, manufactures, sells, and services semiconductor testing components, printed circuit boards, and electronic components and finished products, as well as automatic license plate recognition software and hardware. Additionally, it provides property development and management; system, network, and communications integration; smart buildings and power network; digital information supply and advertisement; property and liability insurance agency; family education; computing equipment installation; consultancy services; data processing; telecommunications engineering; and Internet identity services. Additionally, the company provides software design services and produces and distributes internet content; produces and distributes motion pictures; and provides energy-saving solutions and international circuits, as well as services for electronic parts and mechanically processed items. Chunghwa Telecom Co., Ltd is headquartered in Taipei City, Taiwan.
