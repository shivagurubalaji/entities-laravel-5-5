Bankinter SA (BKT.xmad) Profile

Bankinter SA (BKT.xmad) is a financial services company headquartered in Madrid, Spain. Bankinter SA provides various banking products and services to corporate customers and individuals, and small and medium sized enterprises around the country. The company offers also products such as retail, personal, private, commercial, and corporate banking products, as well as remote banking services. Bankinter SA stock (BKT.xmad) is listed on the Bolsa de Madrid (Madrid Stock Exchange), had a market capitalisation of €3.85B (EUR) as of December 2021, and a €1.39 (EUR) in 2021 yearly revenue. Bankinter is the parent company of 32 companies including EVO Banco, Intermobiliaria, S.A., Avantcard DAC, LDActivos S.L.U., Hispamarket, S.A., Relanza Gestión, S.a, Bankinter Luxembourg S.A.The company employs 6,119 people, operates through a network of 446 offices and has 388 agents. Bankinter SA acquired EVO Banco, incorporating its 452,000 clients and having in total a large portfolio of almost 800,000 clients. The company signed agreement with Logalty in order to provide digital services to Bank’s companies in order to inchance activities and simplify the approach for every customer.
