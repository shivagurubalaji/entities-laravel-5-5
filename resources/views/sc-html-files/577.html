Netapp Inc (NTAP.xnas) Profile

Netapp Inc (NTAP.xnas) is an American cloud data services, storage systems, and software company headquartered in California, the United States. NetApp provides hybrid cloud data services for the management of applications and data both online and physically. Netapp Inc has about 10,500 employees and operates more than 70 office locations in 30 countries worldwide, including Israel, the United States, Australia, the United Kingdom, New Zealand, France, Italy, Germany, Switzerland, Russia, China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Brazil, and Argentina. NetApp has an extensive portfolio of over 60 subsidiary brands, including NetApp, Spinnaker, Network Appliance, Alacritus, Decru, Topio, NetCache, Onaro, Akorri Networks, NA Technology, Sharfnet Israel, and PT NetApp. Netapp Inc stock (NTAP.xnas) is traded on the Nasdaq, is listed as a Standard and Poor’s 500 (S&P 500) component, has a $19.53B (USD) market cap as of November 2021 and $5.74B (USD) in 2021 yearly revenue. The American data-centric software company has extended its partnership with the Ducati Corse motorcycle racing team at the completion of the 2021 MotoGP World Championship race in Valencia, Spain. Netapp Inc Optimized 3D product design in complex development projects by the implementation of a new virtual desktop environment, and automated the racing team’s data workflow before, during and after the race. On November 8 2021, Netapp Inc (NTAP.xnas)completed the acquisition of CloudCheckr.
