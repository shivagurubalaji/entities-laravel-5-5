Salesforce.com Inc Profile

Salesforce.com Inc. (CRM.xnys) is an American cloud-based software company headquartered in California, the United States. Salesforce develops enterprise cloud computing solutions focusing on customer relationship management, known as CRM. It also provides a complementary suite of company applications focused on marketing, analytics, and application development. The company is being traded on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), as a component of the Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA), component of the Standard and Poor’s 100 (S&P-100), and the Standard and Poor’s 500 (S&P-500) and its current market cap is $226.93B (USD). Salesforce’s recent key acquisitions include Slack Technologies (27.7B USD) in 2020 and Tableau (15.3B USD) in 2019. The pandemic has proven to be a positive factor in Salesforce’s share price. Salesforce.com Inc.’s (CRM.xnys) share price rose from $122 (USD) in December 2019 to $271.10 (USD) per share in August 2020. Salesforce appears to have stabilized its share price at around $240 (USD) per share during the first half of 2021.
