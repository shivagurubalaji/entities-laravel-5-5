Siemens Healthineers AG (SHL.xetr) Profile

Siemens Healthineers AG(SHL.xetr) develops, manufactures, and distributes imaging, diagnostic, and advanced therapies products and services to healthcare providers worldwide and operates through three segments; Imaging, Diagnostics, and Advanced Therapies with headquarters in Erlangen, Germany. Siemens Healthineers is connected to the larger corporation, Siemens AG and its the parent company for 64 medical technology companies, among them Varian Medical Systems, Siemens Medical Solutions USA, Inc., Corindus, Inc., Siemens Healthcare Diagnostics GmbH, Siemens Healthcare Inc., Fast Track Diagnostics Ltd, Siemens Healthcare Deutschland, Siemens Healthcare d.o.o. Beograd, Fast Track Diagnostics Luxembourg S.à R.L., Siemens Japan K.K. Siemens Helathinees AG (SHL.xetr) stock is listed on the Frankfurt stock exchange and is a constituent of the DAX. The company has 66,000 employees, operates in 70 countries worldwide and has a market capitalisation of €72.98 (EUR) as of November 2021. Siemens was the first company to combine positron emission tomography (PET) with computed tomography. It affects 90% of leading hospitals through collaboration with researchers, partners, caregivers, and global institutions and almost 3/4 of all clinical decisions by the company’s solutions with €1.5B (EUR) annual investment into research and development for the year 2021. Siemens Helathineers holds 23,000 technical intellectual property rights and more than 14,000 granted patents. For the following years, the company’s purpose is to serve even more customers and patients, fight the most threatening diseases, enable efficient operations, and expand access to care.
