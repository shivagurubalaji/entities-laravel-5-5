Yum! Brands Inc. (YUM) Profile

Yum!Brands is an american fast food corporation founded in 1997, that previously traded under the name Tricon Global Restaurants. It operates the Pizza Hut, Taco Bell, KFC and The Habit Burger Grill brands in all of their locations except China. Yum!Brands is one of the largest fast food companies in the world, operating over 43,000 restaurants in 135 nations. The company owns over 2,000 of the restaurants and the rest are franchised. Despite having brick and mortar businesses the company has remained profitable throughout the pandemic showing their resilience as a company and their ability to adapt with the move to food delivery services. CFD is a financial contract that allows you to trade on the underlying asset price without the need to purchase the actual stock. Investors are trading the difference in the value of a security, between the opening and closing of the trade. You can either hold a long position (Buy) or a short position (Sell). CFDs are considered short-term investments or trades, as CFDs tend to be used within shorter timeframes.
