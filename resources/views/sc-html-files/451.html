Repsol SA (REP.xmad) Profile

Repsol S.A.Spanish pronunciation: [repˈsol]) is a Spanish energy and petrochemical company Headquartered in Madrid. It is engaged in worldwide upstream and downstream activities. In 2020 Forbes Global 2000, Repsol was ranked as the 645th-largest public company in the world. It has more than 24,000 employees worldwide. It is vertically integrated and operates in all areas of the oil and gas industry, including exploration and production, refining, distribution and marketing, petrochemicals, power Products.
