Randstad NV Profile

Randstad NV (RAND.xams) is a Dutch multinational human resource consulting company headquartered in Diemen, Netherlands. RAND focuses in human resource services for temporary and permanent jobs, including contract staffing of professionals and senior managers. Randstad promotes its activities through Randstad, Randstad Care, Tempo Team, Expectra, and "Yacht." Randstad NV employs around 29,000 people, and operates 4,861 branches in more than 39 countries. In 2019, according to Staffing Industry Analysts’ Largest Global Staffing Firms list, Randstad become largest staffing and recruitment company by revenue. In the same year, Google contracted Randstand NV to collect data for its Pixel 4 facial recognition authentication feature from people with dark skin. Randstad NV announced taht the project was temporarily suspended and project leaders were retrained, after the New York Daily News published a report on the project. Randstad NV (RAND.xams) is traded on the Euronext as a constituent of the Amsterdam Exchange Index (AEX) and its current market cap is €11.39B (EUR).
