TotalEnergies SE Profile

TotalEnergies SE (TTE.xnys) is a French multinational integrated oil and gas company headquartered in Paris, France. TotalEnergies is a broad energy company that produces and markets fuels, natural gas, and electricity. TOTALENERGIESSE employs 105,476 people and energy professionals across its facilities in 130 countries around the world. TotalEnergies SE stock (TTE.xnys) is listed on the Euronext, the Frankfurt Stock Exchange (FWB), the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), and as a CAC-40 Index constituent. The French power giant has a $109.36B (USD) or €93.86B (EUR) market cap as of October 20221 and $140.7B (USD) or €120.75 (EUR) in 2020 yearly revenue. In December 2020, the company announced that it is planning to cut 500 voluntary jobs in France. TotalEnergies SE left the American Petroleum Institute lobby due to differences in the shared vision on tackling climate change in January 2021. TotalEnergies SE (TTE.xnys) plans to install 100GW of renewable power generation capacity by 2030, increase its customers to 13M and install 150.000 charge points in Europe by 2025.
