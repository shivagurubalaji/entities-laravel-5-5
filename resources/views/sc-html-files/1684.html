Australian & New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ) Profile

Australian & New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ.xasx) is an Australian multinational banking and financial services company headquartered in Melbourne, Australia. ANZ is currently the fourth-largest financial institution in Australia, serving 8.5 million retail and business customers with activities across 33 markets globally. The Australian banking group runs an extensive portfolio of subsidiaries, with more than 500 branches in Australia, New Zealand and abroad, and employs a workforce of approximately 51,000 employees. Australian & New Zealand Banking Group Ltd (ANZ.xasx) is listed on the Australian Securities Exchange Ltd (ASX), the New Zealand Exchange (NZX), and as a Standard and Poor’s Australian Securities Exchange 200 Index (S&P/ASX 200) constituent. The group has a $79.34B (AUD) market cap, $17.71B (AUD) in 2020 yearly revenues, and $1.04T (AUD) in total assets. ANZ Banking Group has recently partnered with VISA for “buy now pay later” (BNPL) services. Furthermore, ANZ has been shortlisted by the Monetary Authority of Singapore for a competition to overcome problems in developing the central bank’s digital currency (cryptocurrency).
