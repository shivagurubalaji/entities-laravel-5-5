China Life Insurance Co Ltd Profile

China Life Insurance Co Ltd (2628.xhkg) is a Chinese state-owned financial insurance enterprise and annuity products provider headquartered in Beijing, the People’s Republic of China. China Life Insurance offers a wide range of life, accident, and health insurance products and services. China Life has developed a network of more than 8,000 field offices, 4,800 branch offices, 3,000 customer service offices, 87,000 sales outlets, and approximately 717,000 employees. CHINALIFE has a ¥674.29B (CNY) market cap, making it the third-largest insurer globally. It is traded on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), the Shanghai Stock Exchange (SSE), and the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (SEHK).
