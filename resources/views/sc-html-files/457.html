Western Digital Corporation (WDC) Profile

Western Digital Corporation is an American company that designs, manufactures, and sells data technology products, including storage devices, data centre systems, and cloud storage services headquartered in San Jose, California. Western Digital's revenue in 2021 increased 19% quarter-over-quarter and 15% year-over-year to $4.9 billion. The company's improving execution and unique ability to shift bits across broad routes to diverse markets enabled growth in revenue and gross margin and drove strong free cash flow. Client Devices experienced broad-based strength across nearly every product category on a sequential basis. Contributing to this momentum was better than expected demand for notebook and desktop HDDs, as well as flash-based solutions. Moreover, there was robust demand for gaming, smart video, automotive, and industrial applications. The market cap of Western Digital Corporation was 17.90B USD as of September 2021.
