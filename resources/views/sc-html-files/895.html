Texas Instruments Profile

Texas Instruments is an American technology company headquartered in Texas, the United States. TXN designs and manufactures semiconductors and various integrated circuits, which it sells to electronics designers and manufacturers worldwide. TI operates a network of 14 manufacturing sites globally, has approximately 30,000 employees and produces tens of billions of chips each year. Texas Instruments has a portfolio of 80,000 products for over 100,000 customers. Texas Instruments (TXN.xnas) is listed on the NASDAQ as a Nasdaq 100, a Standard & Poor’s 100 (S&P-100) and a Standard & Poor’s 500 (S&P-500) component. TXN has a $179.92B (USD) market cap and $14.38B (USD) yearly revenue. Recently, Texas Instruments introduced the smallest and most accurate transformer technology for designers to cut their power solution size in half for use in high-voltage environments, including electric vehicles (EV), hybrid EVs, motor-drive systems and grid-tied inverters.
