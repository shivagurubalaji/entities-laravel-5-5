FP Markets is seeking people who share the same values and passion for the financial markets. Established in 2005, a constant drive for success has seen FP Markets earn 40+ industry awards including #1 Global Value Forex Broker in consecutive years (2019.20). Professionalism, integrity, and respect are among the core values of FP Markets. They have helped build a foundation of success as a Forex and CFD broker.

We are a dynamic, growing team with headquarters in Sydney, Australia. The operations of FP Markets Group are not limited to Australia with a growing office in Limassol, Cyprus. In searching for the best, we also employ staff in the United Kingdom, Asia, India and beyond. We provide our staff with an exciting working environment that includes opportunities for professional development coupled with a competitive remuneration package.
