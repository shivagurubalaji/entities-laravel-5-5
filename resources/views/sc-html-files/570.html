Dollar Tree Inc (DLTR) Profile

Dollar Tree, formerly known as Only $1.00, is an American discount variety shop chain with multiple price points. It is a Fortune 500 corporation headquartered in Chesapeake, Virginia, that operates 15,115 locations throughout the 48 United States and Canada. It's outlets are backed up by a 24 hour-distribution centre nationwide logistical network. Additionally, the corporation sustains Dollar Bill’s locations and a multi-price point variety chain under the Family Dollar moniker. Dollar Tree is a retailer that operates in the dollar store and low-end retail segments. Each Dollar Tree carries a diverse assortment of merchandise from national, regional, and private-label companies. Health and beauty, food and snacks, party, seasonal décor, housewares, glassware, dinnerware, household cleaning supplies, candy, toys, gifts, gift bags and wrap, stationery, craft supplies, teaching supplies, automotive, electronics, pet supplies, and books are just some of the departments found in a Dollar Tree store. The majority of Dollar Tree locations also sell frozen foods and dairy products, including milk, eggs, pizza, ice cream, frozen dinners, and pre-made baked goods.

Market Cap 31.71B Usd. Dollar Tree’s total assets for the quarter ending October 31, 2021, were $21.390B. Full-Time Employees: 60,217.
