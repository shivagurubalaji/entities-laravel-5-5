ABN AMRO Bank NV Profile

ABN AMRO Bank NV (ABN.xams) is a leading Dutch state-owned bank, subsidiary of ABN AMRO Group with headquarters in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. ABN provides loans, mortgages, and other financial-related services and solutions to companies and individuals, mainly Northwest Europe. ABN AMRO Bank operates over 200 branches and has approximately 17,813 employees, serving more than 6.8 million customers in 15 countries globally. ABN AMRO Bank NV (ABN.xams) is listed on the Euronext Amsterdam has a €5.79B (EUR) market cap and €393M (EUR) 2021 second quarter’s net profits. Earlier this year, ABN agreed to pay $575M (USD) with Dutch prosecutors following a multibillion-dollar money-laundering investigation by the country’s authorities. ABN AMRO Bank NV (ABN.xams) will soon appoint three new Chief Commercial Officers (CCO) for its Executive Board. In addition, ABN AMRO Bank NV (ABN.xams) will simplify its organizational and commercial setup by reorganizing its business structure to three units replacing the current four company segments.
