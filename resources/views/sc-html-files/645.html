Charter Communications Inc (CHTR) Profile

Charter Communications, Inc. serves as a broadband connection and cable operator in the United States, primarily serving residential and commercial clients. The company offers Internet services including security software that safeguards computers against viruses and spyware; in-home Wi-Fi, which offers customers with high-performance wireless routers to help boost their in-home wireless Internet experience; out-of-home WiFi; and Spectrum WiFi services, as well as video services. Additionally, it provides voice communications services via voice over Internet protocol technology, as well as broadband communications solutions for industry and carrier organizations, including Internet access, data networking, fiber connectivity, video media, and business telephone services to cellphone towers and office complexes. Moreover, the company provides mobile services; video programming, static IP and business WiFi, email and encryption, and multi-line telephony services; as well as Web-based managed services, across multiple platforms for networks such as MTV, CNN, and ESPN; sells advertising inventory to local sports and news channels, and offers Audiences Apps to optimize linear inventory. Furthermore, it provides communications products and professional service solutions to mobile and wireline carriers, as well as wholesale data connectivity services to mobile and wireline providers. It also owns and maintains regional sports franchises and news networks. In 41 states, the corporation serves over 31 million clients. Charter Communications, Inc. is headquartered in Stamford, Connecticut.
