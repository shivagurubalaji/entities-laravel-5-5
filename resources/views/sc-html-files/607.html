Vonovia SE (VNA.xetr) Profile

Vonovia SE (VNA.xetr) is a real estate company with 400,000 apartments in Germany, Sweden, and Austria, the majority of the portfolio based in Germany (84%) with headquarters in Bochum, Germany. Vonovia owns 416,236 residential units, 138,176 garages and parking spaces and 6,748 commercial units, which extended to 653 domestic and foreign cities and municipalities. Vonovia SE (VNA.xetr) stock is listed in the DAX 40 (Deutscher Aktienindex (German stock index) and STOXX Europe 600 blue-chip indexes. The company employs 10,345 people, had a €4.4B (EUR) in 2020 yearly revenue and a market cap of €32.02B (EUR) as of November 2021. Vonovia, with the majority of shares in Deutsche Wohnen agreed to sell 14,750 apartments in Berlin to the public sector for €2.46B (EUR) to public housing companies Degewo, Howoge and Berlinovo. The company’s consolidation with Deutsche Wohnen will significantly increase its market share in Berlin as Vonovia holds an overall share of around 87.6% of the voting rights. One part of the merger is the Zukunfts- und Sozialpakt Wohnen (Future and Social Pact for Housing), concluded with the state of Berlin committing to building 13,000 new apartments and implementing a voluntary cap on rents within Berlin finishing 2023. The German landlord reports their contribution to the UN SDG's like CO2 neutrality with every year targets formulating.
