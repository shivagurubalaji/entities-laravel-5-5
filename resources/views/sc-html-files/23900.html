It is possible to transfer your funds directly from another broker into your FP Markets trading account. All you need to do is download our broker transfer form and fill in your details before scanning it and sending it our email address – accountingteamsc@fpmarkets.com – and to your existing broker.

 

Our client service team takes the lead from here and helps you complete the transfer: but bear in mind that it could take up to seven days to complete depending on your existing broker.
