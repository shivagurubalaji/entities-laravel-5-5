ACS Actividades Cons y Serv SA Profile

ACS Actividades Cons y Serv SA (ACS.xmad) is a Spanish construction and services group headquartered in Madrid, Spain. ACS Group focuses mainly on the energy, industrial, and mobility infrastructures. The Spanish Group also engages in civil and engineering construction, all types of services, telecommunications, and management facilities development. ACS Actividades Cons y Serv SA operates a network of more than 40 subsidiary brands, including Dragados, Hochtief, and Flatiron. The Group is present in more than 70 countries globally and has approximately 180.000 employees. ACS Actividades Cons y Serv SA (ACS.xmad) is listed on the Madrid Stock Exchange (BMAD), has a €6.15B (EUR) market cap and about €35B (EUR) in yearly 2020 revenue. On July 27th, 2021, ACS Group announced an increase in its net profit by 4.8% in the first half of 2021 to €351M (EUR). ACS recently sold 80% of its stake in its subsidiary, Hospital De Toledo, for €401M (EUR).
