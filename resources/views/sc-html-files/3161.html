History of AUD/JPY Trading

The AUD/JPY pair is highly correlated with the global economy's risk climate. If the global economic recovery continues, the AUD will remain in demand, assisting in the pair's appreciation. Any symptoms of distress, on the other hand, will push investors into the JPY, resulting in a decline of the AUD/JPY.
