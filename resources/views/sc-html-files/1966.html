Aaron Hill

After completing his Bachelor’s degree in English and Creative Writing in the UK, Aaron was introduced to financial trading—specifically foreign exchange—over a decade ago. Since then, Aaron obtained the CMT Charter (Chartered Market Technician) and was awarded the CFTe (Certified Financial Technician), and remains a passionate student of the markets. In addition to his technical knowledge, he is currently pursuing the CFA Charter (Chartered Financial Analyst).

Aaron provides a daily market technical report that covers key currency markets, as well as a more in-depth weekly technical report. You can also catch Aaron each week, providing detailed webinars on popular trading concepts.

His studies range from the world of portfolio analysis to the world of algorithmic analysis with applications in quantum systems.

He currently coaches thousands of students around the world in both CFD trading and crypto cycle analysis using the live approach format, which allows in a profession such as trading to be close to the student and to monitor every stage of their development.

Every Friday Fabio provides free webinars of high educational value on the FPMarkets educational platform, with thousands of people connected every week.

His educational content together with his 6 years of experience in the markets have earned Fabio a 5-star rating on Trustpilot and a 99% appreciation score on his YouTube content.
