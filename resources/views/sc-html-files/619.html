ContextLogic Inc (WISH) Profile

ContextLogic Inc. is a mobile e-commerce group that operates in Europe, North America, South America, and the rest of the world. The company owns and administers the Wish platform, which links consumers and merchants. Additionally, it offers merchants with marketplace and logistical solutions. San Francisco, California is the company's headquarters. Full-Time Employees: 875 (estimated). Latest stock price as of January 14 2022 2.5300-0.1900 (-6.99%) At close: 04:00 PM EST. Market cap 1.63B USD.

Subsidiaries: Wish Outlet and Wish Express.
