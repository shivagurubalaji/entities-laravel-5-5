Virgin Galactic Holdings, Inc (SPCE) Profile

Virgin Galactic Holdings, Inc. is an American integrated spaceflight company pioneering human spaceflight for private individuals and researchers. It is developing a spaceflight system designed to offer customers/tourists a unique and transformative experience. In February 2019, the project carried three people, including a passenger, on VSS Unity VF-01, with a team member floating within the cabin during a spaceflight that reached 89.9 kilometres (55.9 mi). After two years, on July 11, the company founder Richard Branson and three other employees rode on a flight as passengers, marking the first time a spaceflight company founder has travelled on his own ship into outer space. The company scheduled regular paid passenger service flights to begin in 2022 after completing two more test flights. The market cap of the company is USD 6.33B.
