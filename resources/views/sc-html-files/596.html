Qube Holdings Ltd (QUB.xasx) Profile

Qube Holdings Ltd (QUB.xasx) is an Australian diversified logistics and infrastructure company the largest integrated provider of import and export logistics services in the country, with a market cap of AUD $6.32B as of November 2021. The company operates in 135 locations in Australia, New Zealand and South East Asia with 7,000 employees and holds a 50% of interest in Patrick Terminals, Australia’s leading container terminal operator. In summer 2021, Qube’s Holdings Ltd Independent Director bought AUD $81K worth of stock for AUD $3.25 per share, which boosted their shareholding by 164%. Insiders of the company own 1.8% of Qube Holding shares (QUB.xasx), worth about AUD $112M. The Qube Holding Ltd stock (QUB.xasx) is listed on the Australian Securities Exchange Ltd (ASX).
