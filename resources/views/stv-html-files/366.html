Air France-KLM SA Profile

Air France-KLM SA (AF.xpar) is a Franco-Dutch aviation holding company incorporated under French law headquartered in Paris, France. Since 2004, Air France and KLM have joined forces providing air transportation-related services, including shipments, air cargo solutions, passenger transportation, and aeronautics maintenance. The European airliner has a 44.800 workforce, operating up to 2,300 daily flights with its 554 aircraft fleet. During the 2010-2020 decade, Air France-KLM has transported over 868.430 million passengers across a more than 250-destinations network. Air France-KLM SA stock (AF.xpar) is traded on the Euronext N.V. as a CAC Mid 60 component and has a $2.47B (USD) or €2.12B (EUR) market cap as of October 2021. The airliner’s share price (AF.xpar) has seen a dramatic fall in its value, about a 67.30% decrease in passengers carried, and approximately 57% of losses in revenue since 2018. The pandemics’ uncertainty is neither an ally to Air France-KLM SA’s performance in the aviation industry nor Air France stock in the global stock markets. On October 29th 2021, Air France-KLM SA announced positive earnings for the third quarter due to bookings over the summer and took delivery of its 1st Airbus A220-300s. A significant move in improving Air France-KLM SA‘s financials and the company’s environmental performance.
