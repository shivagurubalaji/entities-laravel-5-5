Crown Castle Intl Corp (CCI) Profile

Crown Castle owns, manages, and rents over 40,000 cellphone towers and roughly 80,000 network miles of fiber that provide small cells and fiber solutions in every major market in the United States. This countrywide network of communications infrastructure links towns and cities to critical data, technology, and cellular service, delivering pertinent data, ideas, and breakthroughs to the people and companies that need them.
