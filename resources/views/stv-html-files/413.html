BASF SE Profile

BASF SE (BAS.xetr) is a German multinational chemical company headquartered in Ludwigshafen, Germany. It is the biggest chemical producer in the world operating in more than 190 countries and more than 390 production sites on all 5 continents. BASF SE is listed on the Frankfurt Stock Exchange (FWB), the London Stock Exchange (LSE), the Zurich Stock Exchange (SIX), and it is also a component of the DAX. BASF SE.’s current market cap is $62.91B (USD). Following a devastating depreciation of its share price back in March 2020, BASF SE (BAS.xetr) seems to be recovering fast. The company’s first two quarterly earnings came out better than expected. The company is due to publish detailed second-quarter figures on July 28, 2021
