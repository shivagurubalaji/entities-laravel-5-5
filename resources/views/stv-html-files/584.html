DexCom Inc (DXCM) Profile

DexCom, Inc. is a medical device business that specializes in the design, development, and marketing of continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) systems in the United States and around the world. The company sells its solutions to persons with diabetes as well as to healthcare practitioners. DexCom G6, an integrated CGM system for diabetes management; Dexcom G7, a future generation G7 CGM system; and Dexcom Share, a remote monitoring system, are among its offerings.

DexCom, Inc. is collaborating and licensing next-generation CGM devices with Verily Life Sciences LLC and Verily Ireland Limited. Endocrinologists, physicians, and diabetes educators are the primary customers for the company's goods.
