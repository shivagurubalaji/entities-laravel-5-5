L'Oreal SA Profile

L'Oreal SA (OR.xpar) is a French cosmetics company headquartered in Paris, France. LOREAL is the world’s leading personal care company focusing on cosmetics, hair care, and perfumes, significantly outperforming the market. The French cosmetics manufacturer operates across its professional products division, consumer products division, L’Oréal Luxe, and the Active Cosmetics division. L'Oreal has over 36 personal care brands, including Maybelline New York, Garnier, NYX Professional Makeup, Yves Saint Laurent, Redken, Kiehl’s, Biotherm, Lancome, Kerastase, Vichy, Diesel, Giorgio Armani, Ralph Lauren, and Valentino. L'Oreal SA (OR.xpar) is listed on the Euronext N.V. as a CAC 40 Index component, has a $220.86B (USD) market cap and €29.87B (EUR) revenue. LOREAL has strongly recovered in North America, while in the Asia Pacific has had a remarkable growth at +23.8%, driven by mainland China at +37.9%. L'Oreal SA e-commerce has grown by +47.2%. LOREAL’s share price had increased by 138.76% in value during the past five years.
