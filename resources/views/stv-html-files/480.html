Zoetis Inc. (ZTS) Profile

Zoetis Inc. (ZTS.xnys) is an American animal health company headquartered in New Jersey, the United States. Zoetis Inc. serves veterinarians, livestock producers, farmers, and companion animal owners in more than 100 countries with 11.300 employees. Once Pfizer’s subsidiary, it is now a completely independent company and the world's largest animal health company. Zoetis Inc. (ZTS.xnys) is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) in Class-A and as a Standard & Poor’s 500 (S&P-500) constituent. The American pet-drug company has a $97.02B market cap and $6.7B (USD) in 2020 revenue, an increase of 7% compared with full-year 2019. Zoetis stock price had an outstanding performance in the global markets during the pandemics. The company’s share price has a 29.58% increase compared to last year.
