Eastman Kodak Co is a firm based in the United States. Print Systems, Enterprise Inkjet Systems, Kodak Software, Brand, Film and Imaging, Advanced Materials and 3D Printing Technology, and Eastman Business Park are the company's segments. The print systems division, which generates the majority of income, provides digital and traditional goods and services to a variety of industries, including commercial printing and book publishing, and accounts for more than half of total sales. The corporation operates on a global scale, with international markets accounting for the majority of revenue.
