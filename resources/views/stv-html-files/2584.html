CaixaBank SA (CABK.xmad) Profile

CaixaBank S.A. (CABK.xmad) is a multinational financial services company headquartered in Valencia, Spain. CaixaBank is the third-largest lender with an asset volume of €685.73B (EUR) and occupies a major European position.CaixaBank has participated in 16 financing operations for renewable energy projects in the UK, with a total capacity of more than 12,000 MW since 2015. The bank has almost 21M customers, more than 6000 branches, 15,000 ATMs and leadership in digital banking with a 71.4% share of digital customers. CaixaBank has a presence in 2,000 municipalities and 350 it is the only represented entity and has offices in Germany, Portugal, Morocco, France, Poland and London. The bank employs 15,911 people, had a market capitalization of €18.74B (EUR) as of December 2021 and a €12.53M (EUR) in 2020 yearly revenue. CaixaBank S.A. stock (CABK.xmad) is listed on the Bolsas y Mercados Españoles (BME). CaixaBank consists of the La Caixa group's universal banking and insurance activities, together with the group’s stakes in Repsol, Telefonica, and its holdings in several other financial institutions. CaixaBank has formalised more than 40 renewable financing operations, particularly in geographies such as Spain, the UK, France, Canada, the United States and Chile, with a volume of €3.25B (EUR) as of December 2021.
