What is Bitcoin CFD?

What is Bitcoin CFD?

Bitcoin CFD trading means that you take a position in the digital currency based on how you think the price of the cryptocurrency will change in the future. Bitcoin CFD trading lets a trader bet on the price of the crypto and take a long or short position, depending on how much they think it will go up or down.
