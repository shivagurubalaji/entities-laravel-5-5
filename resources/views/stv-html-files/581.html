Discovery Inc (DISCA) Profile

Discovery, Inc., a media firm, distributes content in approximately 50 languages through an assortment of distribution systems. It functions through two segments: the United States Networks segment and the International Networks segment.

The company owns and operates the Discovery Channel, HGTV, Food Network, TLC, Animal Planet, Investigation Discovery, Science, Motor Trend, Discovery en Espanol, Discovery Familia, Eurosport, TVN, Discovery Kids, Discovery Family, American Heroes Channel, Travel Channel, Discovery Life, DIY Network, Cooking Channel, Destination America, Great American Country, ID, the Oprah Winfrey Network, Eurosport, DMAX, and Discovery Home & Health. is varied, including subjects such as survival, natural history, exploration, food, travel, heroes, sports, general entertainment, home, adventure, crime, investigation and health.

Additionally, the firm owns and runs production facilities for the creation and distribution of printed and digital media, as well as websites and digital products. It distributes content via a variety of distribution platforms, including pay-television, free-to-air and broadcast television, authenticated GO applications, digital distribution agreements, content licensing agreements and direct-to-consumer subscriptions, through a range of other media, such as brand-aligned Websites, online streaming, mobile devices, video on demand, and broadband channels.
