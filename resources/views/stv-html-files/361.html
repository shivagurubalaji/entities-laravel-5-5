AAC Technologies Holdings Inc Profile

AAC Technologies Holdings Inc (2018.xhkg) is a Chinese audio solution provider headquartered in Shenzhen, the People’s Republic of China. AAC Technologies develops and distributes miniaturized acoustic components, including speakers, microphones, receivers, micro-electro-mechanical system (MEMS) microphones, and other acoustic-related products for smartphones, e-readers, tablets, wearables, ultrabooks, and notebooks. The world-leading company has a workforce of 33,700 employees and operates through its branches across 28 countries in North America, Europe, the United Kingdom, and Asia. AAC Technologies Holdings Inc (2018.xhkg) is listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (SEHK), on the Over the Counter Bulletin Board (OTCBB), and has a $4.99 (USD) market cap. AAC Technologies’ 2020 yearly revenues were $2.48 (USD) while in this year’s first quarter the company announced $662 (USD) revenues, a 22.97% increase compared to last year’s first-quarter results. On June 17, 2021, AAC Technologies Holdings Inc (2018.xhkg) has entered into a strategic partnership with German, Ibeo Automotive Systems GmbH in order to integrate AAC’s expertise in optics and imaging into the development of Ibeo’s future generation of products.
