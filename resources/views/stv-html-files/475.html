Yum China Holdings Inc. (YUMC) Profile

Yum China Holdings Inc. (YUMC.xnys) is an American fast-food company headquartered in Texas, the United States, and operational headquarters in Shanghai, the People’s Republic of China. Yum China operates leading restaurant concepts, including KFC, Pizza Hut, and Taco Bell. Yum China Holdings Inc. operates 11,023 restaurants worldwide with a workforce of 271,000 employees. The company is listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), the Hong Kong Stock Exchange (SEHK), as a Russell-1000 constituent and has a $24.47B (USD) market cap. In 2020, Yum China opened 1.165 new stores while its total revenue increased by 11% year over year to $2.26B (USD). On September 23rd, 2021, Yum China Holdings announced its plan to open 1000 Lavazza cafes in Mainland China and its intention of expanding its operation in smaller cities in China and worldwide.
