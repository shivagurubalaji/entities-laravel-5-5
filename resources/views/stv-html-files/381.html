ADYEN Profile

Adyen NV (ADYEN.xams) is a Dutch online payment provider headquartered in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. Adyen provides a single payments platform globally where merchants and enterprises can perform e-commerce, mobile, and point-of-sale payments. The Dutch online banking provider operates 24 offices across 20 countries, has a 2300 workforce, and a network of 3500 businesses worldwide that has processed a €303.6B (EUR) net volume through Adyen’s online payment platform and services in 2020. Adyen NV (ADYEN.xams) is listed on the Euronext as an Amsterdam Exchange Index (AEX) constituent, has a €73.14B (EUR) market cap, and €684M (EUR) in 2020 yearly revenues. ADYEN has shown high resilience during the pandemic, supported by the expected rise of e-commerce and the company’s successful adaptation to changing circumstances, including working remotely. Recently, the company has launched Planet for purchase carbon footprint offsets, Score with GoFundMe for malicious platform users identification, and started issuing the Takeaway Pay Card for Just Eat Takeaway.com employees.
