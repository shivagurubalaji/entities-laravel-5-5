Starbucks Corporation Profile

Starbucks Corporation (SBUX.xnys) is an American multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery reserves headquartered in Washington, the United States. As the world's largest coffeehouse chain, the Starbucks Corporation operates 32,660 stores in 83 countries with more than 340.000 employees globally. It is traded as a Nasdaq-100, Standard and Poor’s 100 (S&P-100) and Standard and Poor’s 500 component and its markets cap is currently $140.18B (USD). COVID-19 global strict measures and lockdowns caused Starbucks to suffer a 10% sales decrease. In order to overcome the global crisis Starbucks announced the closing of 400 locations in the United States and Canada in 2020, and tried to have rent reductions from landlords. Starbucks Corporation recently focused on a global expansion of its business, while investing in pandemic’s digital-order era. Starbucks (SBUX.xnys) has shown some impressive skills of recovery during the first 2 quarters of 2021.
