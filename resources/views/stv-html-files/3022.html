Copy Trading FAQ

What is Copy Trading ?

Copy Trading allows traders to find, follow and copy successful traders automatically

How can I become a Social Trading Strategy Provider?

It only takes a few steps to become a provider after filling the relevant form. The creation of the strategy will be completed within 24 hours.

How can I start copying?

Signing up for Social Trading with FP Markets is a swift process thanks to a streamlined procedure that can be completed in three easy steps:

1. Open a MetaTrader 4 or MetaTrader 5 account
2. Choose the Provider(s) that you wish to copy
3. Activate and Start Copy Trading

How I can choose whom to copy?

Finding a suitable strategy to copy is the most important component of Social Trading. We provide an in depth breakdown of analytics related to each strategy, these include return on investment, drawdown, instruments traded and much more. All our strategies can be reviewed on the Social Trading Ratings tab.

How does Copy Trading work?

There is no need to build your own trading strategy or conduct research on forex markets. Copy trading removes the complexities of trading forex and CFDs. Once you find a suitable provider, simply activate the strategy, set your risk parameters, and trades will start copying on your account automatically.

How to I fund my copy trading account?

Copy trading accounts are funded in the same way any other account would be funded via the FP Markets Client Portal. Funds can also be transferred from any of your trading accounts, to an account connected to the Social Trading service.

Why can't I activate my strategy?

You may not be able to follow a strategy because of one of the following reasons: 1) Your account does not meet the balance criteria of your preferred strategy. 2) Your profile has not been fully verified/approved. 3) Your trading account is archived.

Are there any fees involved?

There is no fee applied to start copying, performance fees are only applied on profitable trades.Once a Trading Interval ends, the Social Trading software initiates payment of Performance fees to each provider for an individual follower. The calculation is based on the trading results for the ended trading interval, on the performance fee percentage and on the calculation mode.

How I can stop copying?

To stop copying, you need to Unsubscribe. Risk management triggers also launch the Unsubscribe action. Unsubscribing archives a subscription. Suspending a subscription stops copying as well, but keeps its parameters editable for the follower. For example, the follower has realized that their strategy is not suitable for copying from the provider, and they need some time to come up with a new strategy. While in process, the subscription may be suspended to avoid copying unintended trades. Once finished, the follower should press Activate again to start copying, but unlike the first activation, repeated activations don’t provide an option to copy the provider's current positions.

How can I disable my strategy?

To disable your Strategy, you will need to send a request by email to partners@fpmarkets.com and request the closure.
