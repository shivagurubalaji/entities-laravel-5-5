Tesla Inc Profile

Tesla Inc. (TSLA) is an American electric and clean energy automobile manufacturer based in California, the USA. Tesla Motors have entered the Stock Markets filing an initial public offering (IPO) underwritten by Goldman Sachs, J. P. Morgan, Deutsche Bank Securities, and Morgan Stanley in 2010, issuing 13.3M shares at $17.00 (USD) per share. A year later, Tesla Inc’s (TSLA) shares were been sold at the opening price of $4.92 (USD) per share. The company’s all-time high closing price was $883.09 (USD) per share on January 26 2021 and has a market cap of $654.01B (USD) marking a 17,579.69% all-time stock up. Tesla Inc. is being traded as a component of NASDAQ-100, the Standard and Poor’s 100 (S&P-100), and Standard and Poor’s 500 (S&P-500). Recently, Tesla’s share price has lost some 0.3% of its value, because the company’s new German plant won’t be producing when expected from Tesla’s CEO Elon Musk. Nothing is to be taken for granted with rival electronic vehicle manufacturers still on the rise and sinking vehicle demand. Nevertheless, Tesla Inc. (TSLA) is undoubtedly a stock to keep an eye on during 2021.
