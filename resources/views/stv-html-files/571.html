DocuSign Inc (DOCU) Profile

In the United States and globally, DocuSign, Inc. delivers cloud-based software. The company offers an e-signature technology that enables organizations to create, sign, act on, and manage contracts digitally. Additionally, it provides CLM, which automates workflows throughout the agreement process; Insights, which leverages artificial intelligence (AI) to search for and evaluate agreements based on legal ideas and terms; and Gen for Salesforce, which enables sales reps to generate agreements automatically with a few mice clicks directly from within Salesforce; Negotiate for Salesforce, which enables approvals, document comparisons, and version control; Analyzer, which explains what clients are signing before they sign; and CLM, which enables AI-driven contract lifecycle management.

The company offers Guided Forms, which enable the completion of difficult forms through an interactive, step-by-step procedure; Click that supports agreements with standard conditions and consents that do not require signatures; Identify, which enables customers to collect signatures and payment; Standards-Based Signatures, which supports signatures including digital certificates; Payments, which enables customers to collect signatures and money; and eNotary, which permits the execution of electronic notarial actions.

It offers industry-specific cloud offerings such as Rooms for Real Estate, which enables brokers and agents to manage the entire real estate transaction digitally; Rooms for Mortgage, which provides a digital workspace for creating and closing mortgages; FedRAMP, an authorized version of DocuSign eSignature for federal government agencies in the United States; and life sciences modules that support compliance with electronic signature practices. The company sells its items directly, through partners, and online. It provides services to enterprises, commercial enterprises, and small businesses. San Francisco, California is the company's headquarters.
