Spark Infrastructure Group (SKI) Profile

Market Cap 10.073B AUD
SPARK INFRASTRUCTURE RE LIMITED is located in Sydney, NEW SOUTH WALES, Australia and is part of the Electric Power Generation, Transmission and Distribution Industry. SPARK INFRASTRUCTURE RE LIMITED has 20 total employees across all of its locations and generates $5.13 million in sales (USD).

Spark Infrastructure Level 29 225 George Street Sydney NSW 2000 Australia

Spark Infrastructure Group is an Australian investment fund which owns and manages a portfolio of electricity infrastructure assets. The company debuted on the Australian Securities Exchange on 16 December 2005 with the stock symbol SKICA Subsidiaries: Powercor Australia, Spark Infrastructure Trust.
