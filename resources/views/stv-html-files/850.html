LVMH Group SA Profile

LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton SE (MC.xpar) is a French Holding multinational conglomerate specialising in luxury goods headquartered in Paris, France. LVMH is the 2021 most valuable company in Europe, having a portfolio of 60 subsidiaries and operating a network of 75 luxury brands, including Loro Piana, Emilio Pucci, HUBLOT, Christian Dior, TAG Heuer, Givenchy, Marc Jacobs, Donna Karan, Stella McCartney, Kenzo, Celine, Princess Yachts, and Bulgari. LVMH employs approximately 83,000 people operating over 2,400 stores globally. LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton SE stock (MC.xpar) is traded on the Euronext NV, as a CAC-40 Index constituent, has a €342B (EUR) market cap as of November 2021, and €14B (EUR) in 2020 yearly revenues. This sum makes LVMH the world's 17th most valuable company by market cap. Following the company’s attempts of further expansions into the jewellery sector and the Asian Pacific market, it has completed the purchase of Tiffany & Co in January 2021 and surged in China and other parts of Asia. Most recently, LVMH Moet Hennessy Louis Vuitton SE announced a 38% rise in sales, €7.4B (EUR), of the corporation’s fashion and leather goods divisions.
