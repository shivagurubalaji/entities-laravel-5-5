Cerner Corp (CERN) Profile

CGN Power Co., Ltd. is a company that invests in companies that make and sell electricity. Sales of electricity through nuclear energy are part of the business of the Nuclear Power Operation and Sale of Electricity and Related Technical Services group. People in this part of the business do things like build nuclear power plants and design projects, provide technical and training services, and sell machinery and other goods. Furthermore, the company is also in the business of investing, exporting, and importing. Headquarters Shenzhen, Guangdong, China
