Beyond Meat Inc (BYND) Profile

Beyond Meat Inc (BYND.xnas) is a company that produces plant-based meat substitutes headquartered in Los Angeles, California, United States. The company’s initial products include beyond burgers, beyond sausages, beyond meatballs and beyond mince. Beyond Meat products are available in approximately 118,000 retail and foodservice outlets in over 80 countries worldwide, and the company employs 472 people. The Beyond Meat Inc stock (BYND) is listed on the NASDAQ, had a market capitalisation of $3.89B (USD) as of January 2022 and $406.8M (USD) in 2020 yearly revenues. In early 2022, McDonald’s rolled out a test of its plant-based burger in 600 stores across the San Francisco and Dallas-Fort Worth areas. The collaboration with Beyond Meat started earlier in November 2021 when it sold the McPlant in 8 U.S restaurants in Texas, Lowa, Louisiana, and California. McDonald’s customers will be able to find the McPlant starting February 2022 for a limited time while supplies last.
