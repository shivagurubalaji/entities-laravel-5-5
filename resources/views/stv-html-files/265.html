Spotify Technology SA Profile

Spotify Technology SA (SPOT.xnys) is a Swedish audio streaming and media services provider headquartered in Stockholm, Sweden. Spotify Technology SA offers through its app digital copyright restricted recorded music and podcasts from record labels and media companies. Having raised its premium subscribers from 130 million in 2020 to 158 million in 2021, and with over 356 million monthly total active users, Spotify is the largest music streaming provider on a global scale.
