State Street Corp (STT) Profile

State Street Corporation is an American financial services and bank holding company headquartered at One Lincoln Street in Boston with operations worldwide. It is the second-oldest continually operating United States Bank. It is one of the largest asset management companies in the world. It is the largest custodian bank in the world. Its products include Investment management Stock trading, Foreign exchange market securities lending.

Total assets 314,71 Billion USD

The company is ranked 247th on the Fortune 500 as of 2019.

State Street is a leading provider of financial services, including investment servicing, investment management, and investment research and trading. With approximately $32 trillion in assets under custody and administration and $2.7 trillion assets under management as of March, State Street operates globally in more than 100 geographic markets and employs more than 40,000 worldwide Market Cap 34.29 B USD.
