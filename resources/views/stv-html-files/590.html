CVS Health Corp (CVS) Profile

CVS Health Corporation provides health services in the United States. The company's Pharmacy Services segment offers pharmacy benefit management solutions, including plan design and administration, formulary management, retail pharmacy network management, mail order pharmacy, specialty pharmacy and infusion, clinical, and disease and medical management services. It serves employers, insurance companies, unions, government employee groups, health plans, prescription drug plans, Medicaid managed care plans, plans offered on public health insurance and private health insurance exchanges. Other health benefit plan providers and people. This section operates specialist retail pharmacies; specialty mail-order dispensing, and compounding pharmacies; plus, infusion and enteral nutrition service branches. Its Retail/LTC sector sells prescription and OTC medications, consumer health and beauty items, and personal care products, as well as health care services through its MinuteClinic walk-in treatment centers. Retail/LTC distributes prescription and over-the-counter drugs, retail health and beauty products, and personal care products, as well as health care services via its MinuteClinic walk-in treatment locations.
