Corn History

The corn was a human invention as this plant does not exist naturally in the wild. It can survive if planted and protected by humans. Most scientists believe that corn was developed by Mexicans at least 7000 years ago. The Corn Futures contract is the world’s largest agricultural futures contract and started trading in 1877 at the Chicago Board of Trade.

When is the best season to trade corn?

The corn market is open all year long, but as corn is seasonal its high trading season is mostly during the summer period. Corn is distributed and sold during the winter months, which leaves the summer to speculate the growth value, estimated numbers and more. The first and most influential speculations were published in March by the US department of agriculture (USDA). They provide the expected amount of acres planted by the farmers which naturally will impact the corn price in the markets.

What can move the price of corn?

The price of corn commodity can be affected by factors such as changing weather conditions, distribution costs, geopolitics, global health issues and the strength of the US dollar. Also, the price of corn is moved by factors that relate to supply and demand.

Corn trading FAQ

What are the keys to trading corn futures?

What are the keys to
trading corn futures?

It is important for traders to pay attention to the seasons and the weather. Traders and corn farmers should have the same mindset. In the winter months, action in the corn markets is fairly muted. When the spring arrives the market starts again. Keep in mind that throughout the summer period trading can be volatile as every weather report in the corn-producing regions has the potential to move corn prices in one direction or another abruptly.

Why is corn trading significant for traders?

Why is corn trading
significant for traders?

Corn can be a good choice for those who want to diversify their trading and also it can be profitable during summer as the prices can reach their highest levels. Trading is still volatile throughout the summer, with prices reaching lows in October and November. In addition, corn is a play on the increasing use of biofuels, and with countries globally working towards increasing their use of ethanol over crude oil, corn stands to benefit greatly in the coming years.

What is the right corn trading strategy?

What is the right corn trading strategy?

There are two options of analysis for traders when it comes to corn trading. The first option is technical analysis and the second is fundamental analysis. However, as corn is seasonal, the most effective strategies tend to be technical analysis strategies. Traders should still keep an eye on things like the weather around key corn-growing regions and the USA reports, as they can have a strong impact on corn prices.
