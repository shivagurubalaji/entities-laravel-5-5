Coty Inc (COTY) Profile

Coty Inc. and its subsidiaries manufacture, market, distribute, and sell beauty goods globally. The company distributes luxury fragrances, skincare, and colour cosmetics products through a network of prestigious retailers, which include perfumeries, retail locations, e-commerce sites, direct-to-consumer internet sites, and duty-free shops, under the Alexander McQueen, Burberry, Bottega Veneta, Calvin Klein, Cavalli, Chloe, Davidoff, Escada, Gucci, Hugo Boss, Jil Sander, Joop!, Kylie Jenner, Lacoste. Furthermore, the company sells mass colour cosmetics, fragrance, skincare, and body care products primarily through megastores, supermarket chains, drug stores, pharmacies, mid-tier department stores, conventional food and drug retailers, and e-commerce retailers under the Adidas, Beckham, Biocolor, Bozzano, Bourjois, Bruno Banani, CoverGirl, Jovan, Max Factor, Mexx, Monange, Nautica, Paixao, Rimmel, Risque, Additionally, Coty Inc. sells its goods in about 150 countries and territories through third-party distributors. The company is headquartered in New York City. Cottage Holdco B.V. owns Coty Inc.
