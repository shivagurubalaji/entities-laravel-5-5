CGN Power Co Ltd (1816.xhkg) Profile

Cerner Corporation, through its subsidiaries, provides information technology solutions and technology-enabled services in health care in the United States and globally. Cerner Millennium architecture is a person-centric computing framework that encompasses clinical, financial, and management information systems. It enables providers to access an individual's electronic health record (EHR) at the point of care and organises and delivers information to physicians, nurses, laboratory technicians, pharmacists, front- and back-office professionals, and consumers. Additionally, it offers the Health Intent platform, a cloud-based platform for aggregating, transforming, and reconciling data across the continuum of care, as well as CareAware, an EHR-independent platform for connecting health care devices to EHRs. Additionally, the company provides a range of clinical and financial healthcare information technology solutions, as well as departmental and care coordination services. Additionally, it offers technology-enabled services such as implementation and training, remote hosting, operational management, revenue cycle management, support and maintenance, health care data analysis, real-world evidence, clinical process optimisation, transaction processing, employer health centres, employee wellness programs, and third-party administrator services, as well as complementary hardware and devices for third parties. Integrated delivery networks, physician groups and networks, managed care organisations, hospitals, medical centres, reference laboratories, home health agencies, blood banks, imaging centres, pharmacies, pharmaceutical manufacturers, employers, governments, and public health organisations are among the company's customers. Cerner is headquartered in North Kansas City, Missouri.
