Aena SME SA (AENA) Profile

Aena SME SA (AENA.xmad) is a Spanish state-owned company that manages general interest airfields headquartered in Madrid, Spain. The world’s leading airport manager by passenger traffic operates through a portfolio of three subsidiaries, including Aena Internacional, Aena Desarrollo Internacional SA, and London Luton Airport Operations Ltd. The Spanish airport operator employs approximately 8700 people, manages 48 airfields in Spain, and participating in the management of another 23 airports abroad. Aena SME SA (AENA.xmad) is listed on the Madrid stock Exchange (BMAD), has a €21.442B (EUR) market cap and €489.31M (EUR) in 2021 revenues. In 2020, AENA had an enormous pandemic impact watching its numbers dropping by 72% to 1,101,185 operations and by 53.36% to 76,064,322 passenger traffic, compared to 2019 results. Aena SME SA is planning to build a 28-hectare plot near Madrid-Barajas for air and ground freight.
