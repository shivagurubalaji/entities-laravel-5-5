Amadeus IT Group SA (AMS) Profile

Amadeus IT Group SA (AMS.xmad) is a Spanish travel and tourism IT provider headquartered in Madrid, Spain. Amadeus was founded by Lufthansa, Air France, Iberia, and Scandinavian Airlines in 1987 for the provision of computer reservations systems and IT solutions for online travel agencies. It is currently employing 19,000 people and operating in 195 countries. In 2018, Amadeus IT acquired TravelClick, a cloud-based service provider for the hotel industry, for €1.31B (EUR). Amadeus IT Group SA (AMS.xmad) is listed on the Madrid Stock Exchange (BMAD), has a €27.4B (EUR) market cap and €2.21B (EUR) in 2020 annual revenues. Amadeus has partnered with IBM to develop digital health verification technology for the COVID-19 health documentation.
